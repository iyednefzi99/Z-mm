\chapter{Appendix: Physical structure of a hive}
%==============================================================================
For the record, a movable-frame hive is made up, from bottom to top, of the
following elements: floor / landing board, body (base) holding the brood frames,
queen excluder, one or more supers holding the honey-harvest frames, crown board
and roof. This structure justifies the composition rules (mandatory body, up to
5 supers, 1 to 10 frames per element) adopted in the business model.
